Privacy Policy for G Task Express

Effective Date: May 21, 2024

G Task Express ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension ("Extension") to manage your Google Tasks.

Information We Collect

Google Tasks Data: When you use our Extension, you will be asked to grant permission to access your Google Tasks data. This includes reading your task lists and creating new tasks. We only access this data to provide the functionality of the Extension.

OAuth Tokens: To interact with the Google Tasks API, our Extension uses OAuth 2.0 tokens provided by Google. These tokens are stored locally on your browser and are used to authenticate API requests.

How We Use Information

Google Tasks Management: We use the data we collect solely to provide the functionality of the Extension. This includes reading your task lists, displaying them to you, and allowing you to create new tasks.

No Backend Storage: We do not store any of your Google Tasks data on our servers. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

Information Sharing

Third-Party Services: We do not share your Google Tasks data with third parties. The data accessed by the Extension is used only within the context of providing the functionality of the Extension.

Google API Services: The Extension uses Google API services to access your Google Tasks data. Your use of these services is subject to Google's privacy policies.

Data Security

Local Storage: OAuth tokens and any other data accessed by the Extension are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

No Additional Security Measures: Our Extension relies on standard security measures provided by the browser and Google API services. We do not implement additional technical or organizational security measures beyond these standard practices.

User Choices and Controls

Revoke Access: You can revoke the Extension's access to your Google Tasks data at any time through your Google account settings.

Uninstallation: If you uninstall the Extension, all locally stored data, including OAuth tokens, will be deleted from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website or through the Extension. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at gtaskexpress@gmail.com.
